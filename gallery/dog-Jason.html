Put clearscreen() as the first line in a cell (after the import command) to re-run turtle commands in the cell

clearscreen()
initializeTurtle()
speed(10)
pensize(2)

# 小狗头部
penup()
goto(0, -30)
pendown()
fillcolor("saddlebrown")
begin_fill()
circle(60)
end_fill()
